About Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking ( Formerly known as Maruti Insurance Broking )

Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking - Bringing Smiles to Vehicle Owners, since 2002

In the year 2000-2001, Maruti Suzuki observed that there is a great need to enhance customers’ value proposition in the field of Motor Insurance and hence decided to get into the business of Motor Insurance distribution. Customer convenience in every aspect of the business along with fairness and transparency in policy issuance and claims were taken as the objectives.

In year 2002, through corporate agency route, Maruti Suzuki ventured in its Insurance distribution venture to provide best in class Insurance Services to the customers. The business set newer benchmarks of success and has created delight for 4 Cr customers in its working span of over one and a half decades.

In year 2012, Maruti Suzuki migrated to Broking through its joint venture company, Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking Private limited. Today, with over 8 million “Happy Customers”, Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking ( Formerly known as Maruti Insurance Broking ) is the largest Insurance Broking Services provider in India ensuring complete peace of mind for its customers, fulfilling their motor insurance needs on end to end basis.

The Pan-India presence ensures efficient service, across 450+ cities from the highest mountains of Leh to the least inhabited islands of Andaman, from the barren desert of Kutch to the green valleys of Imphal.

Careers

At Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking , we provide a strong, equitable and equal opportunity foundation for individuals to build their careers. Our vibrant culture mirrors the multi-dimensional backgrounds of our people.
Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking is changing the way motor insurance is brought in India, one car at a time. We provide Maruti Suzuki car owners the choice to opt for a policy from among 20 of India’s top auto insurance companies, all from the unified Maruti Suzuki Insurance Broking platform. Considering the number of Maruti Suzuki cars sold every day, this means that thousands of customers access our services on a daily basis.

What has always set us apart is our range of Maruti Suzuki customers across
India – from the farmers of Kashmir to resort operators in Andaman and Nicobar, our cars are used by people across the entire economic spectrum. We understand and appreciate the faith our fellow Indians have put in us and in turn strive to provide them the very best of services.

We love what we do and are extremely proud of helping our customers. Customer-focused service is ingrained into our value system and we look for dedicated people who love bringing smiles on customers’ faces.
